1994 USA World Cup

The is the first time that the newly reunified German national team participated in the FIFA World Cup.

Just like last World Cup, the German national team performed well during the qualification stage. They had two wins (1-0 Bolivia and 3-2 South Korea) and one draw (1-1 Spain). During the knockout stage, Germany beat Belgium 3-2, but lost to Bulgaria in the Quarter-finals game (1-2).
