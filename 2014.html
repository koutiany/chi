2014 Brazil World Cup

Germany won the World Cup for the fourth time in history! It is also the first World Cup they won after the reunification!

Germany finished the qualification stage with 7 points, two wins (4-0 Portugal, 1-0 United States) and one draw (2-2 Ghana). They knock out Algeria with the difference of one goal (2-1). In the Quarter-finals they outperformed France (1-0) and came to the semi-final stage where they played against Brazil who had won the World Cup five times. The game ended in an unexpected way where Brazil lost to Germany 1-7. In the final, Germany defeated Argentina for the fourth time in the past 14 years during the World Cup knockout stage (1-0).

Coach Löw brought almost the same team as four years ago. From the map, we can see the state North Rhine-Westphalia contributed a lot of good football players who eventually were selected to perform on the international stage. Both Mesut Özil and Manuel Neuer come from Gelsenkirchen. Julian Draxler grew up in Gladbeck, Kevin Großkreutz in Dortmund, and Mats Hummels in Bergisch Gladbach. Shkodran Mustafi was born in Bad Hersfeld, Hesse, to an Albanian familz originally from Gostivar, Macedonia. The team 2014 consisted of at leasat six players with multicultural backgrounds and it won the World Cup for the first time after the reunification. The cooperation they presented on the pitch earned compliments from all over the world. Fans today stil talk about the huge win they had against Brazil!

The German national team celebrated winning of the World Cup in Berlin!
