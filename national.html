Die Deutsche Mannschaft

Since 1908, the German national football team has represented Germany in international competition. In the past hundred years, this team has won the World Cup four times (1954, 1974, 1990, 2014) and had highest Elo ranking of first place in total six years, with a current ranking at the 6th place. From 1945 to 1990, due to the existence of German Democratic Republic and Federal Republic of Germany, there were two national teams. This project intends to only focus on the players who were on the national football teams after the reunification in 1990.
